About us

Golden Horizon Bank makes installment loans at all
of our full-service bank locations, with friendly,
helpful loan officers. Golden Horizon Bankis an Indian nationalised banking
and financial services company. It is
under the ownership of Ministry of Finance,
Government of India. It is the fourth largest
nationalised bank in India, with 132 million customers,
a total business of US$218 billion, and a
global presence of 100 overseas offices.
